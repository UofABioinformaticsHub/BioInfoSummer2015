General Information

The BioInfoSummer series introduces bioinformatics and mathematical and computational biology to advanced undergraduate and postgraduate students, researchers and professionals working in a variety of fields.

Established in 2003, BioInfoSummer is a key training event in Australia, bringing together people from all disciplines to share current research and developments in Bioinformatics.

Who: The course is aimed at researchers with a minimal knowledge of R. THere is n alternative session for those who no experience of R.
